Welcome to Tutor Bot

The Tutor Bot app is a practical scheduling application to be used by teachers and students for scheduling classes.

Why Tutor Bot?

Tutor Bot takes the hassel out of scheduling so that teachers and students can focus on what matters most, learning.

the Tutor Bot Platform

The Tutor Bot is platform for managing teacher and student schedules with integrated services like push notifications, native builds, user auth, and live updating.
